Firestick Arsonist Device linked to Unabomber case

A Firestick incendiary device is soaked in a chemical then placed in a bucket containing another chemical. When the stick is later withdrawn from the bucket the two chemicals mixing with the air cause spontaneous ignition. The ferocious flame spatters burning metal drops which pass quickly through skin and bone causing debilitating burns. One individual was permanently paralyzed in a Firestick attack in New York City in 1994.

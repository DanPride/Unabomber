15297 Bohlman Road Garage Diagram circa 1992

During these events the house at 15297 Bohlman Road was listed for Sale. Numerous Realtors would visit the property, often taking snapshots of the property. It is likely that photos of the garage displaying numerous bomb parts from the Unabomber Bombings may exist in local realtor files. Caution must be advised for any possesor of such photos given the violent history of the case for insiders.
